About Cairo

Cairo, known as "The City of a Thousand Minarets," is the largest city in the Arab world and Africa. With a history spanning thousands of years, it serves as Egypt's political, cultural, and economic center. The city blends ancient Egyptian, Coptic Christian, and Islamic heritage with modern development. From the Pyramids of Giza on its outskirts to the medieval streets of Islamic Cairo, the metropolis offers an incredible diversity of experiences. While Cairo can be chaotic and overwhelming at first, its energy, hospitality, and incredible monuments make it an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking to understand Egypt's past and present.

Best Time to Visit Cairo

The ideal time to visit Cairo is during the cooler months from October to April. Winters are
mild and pleasant, while summers can be extremely hot and dry.
