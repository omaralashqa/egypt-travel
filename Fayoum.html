About Fayoum

Fayoum is a fascinating blend of nature and history. Home to ancient fossils, stunning lakes, and charming villages, it offers a peaceful escape from city life. Whether you're interested in adventure, history, or relaxation, Fayoum has something special for every traveler.

Best Time to Visit Fayoum

The best time to visit Fayoum is from October to April when the weather is cool and pleasant.

October - November

Perfect for exploring nature and outdoor activities.

December - February

Mild winter temperatures make sightseeing comfortable.

March - April

Ideal for visiting lakes and enjoying the desert scenery.
